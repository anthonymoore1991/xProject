About

Hi my name is Anthony Moore and I am a web developer and designer based in Dublin. I am currently studying BSc honours in computing in the national college of Ireland, I am half way through my first year. I am also available for some freelance work, so if you are interested in hearing from me please leave a message and I will get back to you at my earliest convince. I do have some experience in web design as I have already done a level 5 P.L.C course in web design. During my time in that part time course I learned basic HTML and inline CSS I also built a web site using Adobe Dreamweaver. Since I have started here in NCI I have increased my skills in HTML, external CSS, JavaScript and JQuery. I plan to incorporate all of these skills to further develop them in all projects in and outside of college.

At secondary level I have passed both Junior and Leaving certificate. When I finished secondary school I was not sure what profession I would have liked to pursue a career in. I decided to do a level 5 certificate in Construction, Technology and Architecture. As a result of Ireland being hit by the recession it would have been very tough to get a job in that sector. I decided to do a course in Web Design which I would come to love and was also the inspiration I needed to apply for a BSc Honours in Computing at the National College of Ireland. I am currently half way through my first year and really enjoying my time at NCI.
I have been working part-time in retail department store Debenhams where I have had gained and developed excellent customer service and communication skills.
